Rules

Getting Started Honor Code

This is a fun competition aimed at helping to build new ways to analays AIRR-seq data and to create novel ways to classify sequences and repertoires.

One account per participant

You cannot sign up to the competition from multiple accounts and therefore you cannot submit from multiple accounts.

code sharing outside teams

Privately sharing code data outside of teams is permitted and welcome. It’s okay to share code to all participants on the forums.

Team Mergers

Team mergers are allowed and can be performed by the team leader. In order to merge, the combined team must have a total submission count less than or equal to the maximum allowed as of the merge date. The maximum allowed is the number of submissions per day multiplied by the number of days the competition has been running.

Team Limits

The maximum team size is 5.

Submission Limits

You may submit a maximum of 2 entries per week.

Start Date: September 10, 2022

Competition End Date: March 10, 2023
